**Just in time for the 13 Colonies Special Event (July 1–8).** This release turns POTACAT into your scoreboard for the hunt — an ongoing checklist that remembers what you've worked so you can focus only on the stations you still need.

## 📋 13 Colonies progress tracker

Track your run at all 16 stations — **K2A–K2M** plus the bonus **WM3PEN**, **GB13COL**, and **TM13COL** — right inside POTACAT:

- **An ongoing checklist that sticks.** Work a colony station and it's ticked off automatically from your log. Your progress now **survives restarts** — a fix for checkmarks that used to disappear when you relaunched during the event.
- **Mark stations you worked elsewhere.** Logged one on paper or in another program? **Click any station to check it off by hand.** Auto-detected QSOs stay locked to your log; manual ticks are yours to toggle.
- **Easier to get to.** The banner's **Progress** button now opens the event you're actually looking at, and when you're tracking more than one event (say, 13 Colonies *and* ARRL America 250) a row of **tabs** lets you flip between boards. On a short window the board **scrolls** instead of running off the top.

Turn it on under **Spots → Events → 13 Colonies**, and watch the "worked / 16" count climb.

## 📱 Smoother tuning from the ECHOCAT phone app

Spinning the VFO on your phone now updates the frequency readout **immediately** while you turn, instead of only catching up when you stop. The desktop echoes each tuned frequency back the instant it's received rather than waiting on the next radio poll.

## 🛠️ Fixes & polish

- Corrected the 13 Colonies event window so the full run through **0400 UTC July 8** counts.
- Assorted rig-control and reliability improvements under the hood.
