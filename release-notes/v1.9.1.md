**Hotfix for v1.9.0.** Two ECHOCAT fixes — recommended for everyone using the phone app.

## 📱 Pairing a new device now works

In 1.9.0 the **"Pair request" popup came up blank** — no device name or fingerprint, a frozen countdown, and the **Approve button did nothing** — so you couldn't pair a new phone from the desktop. A timing race delivered the request details to the popup before it was ready to receive them (it only showed up in the packaged installer, not in development). The popup now reliably shows the device name, platform, address, and certificate fingerprint, and Approve/Deny work as intended.

## ⌨️ CW macros sync from phone to desktop

CW macros you **add or edit on the ECHOCAT phone app now appear on the desktop** (the macro bar refreshes live), and stay in sync both directions. Previously phone edits were stored but never shown on the desktop.
