**Headline:** v1.9.0 makes your **FlexRadio just work** — POTACAT and **SmartSDR or AetherSDR** are now fully interchangeable, with no settings to flip — and brings **1-touch connecting** from the **ECHOCAT** phone app (iOS *and* Android). Plus **unlimited CW macros** and a quieter, more reliable shack.

## 📻 SmartSDR + AetherSDR, used interchangeably

Run POTACAT direct to your Flex with **nothing else open**, then bring up **SmartSDR or AetherSDR** to watch the waterfall — and POTACAT keeps working the whole time. It now hands the radio back and forth automatically, in **both directions** and with **either** client:

- **Open SmartSDR/AetherSDR while running** → POTACAT steps aside cleanly and follows the slice you're working (no more "In use" lockout).
- **Close it** → POTACAT reclaims the radio and keeps going.
- **AetherSDR** (the Linux-native Flex client) is now a first-class citizen alongside SmartSDR.

**Hear the band the way you want:** in direct mode POTACAT can keep the radio's own speaker silent while feeding full audio to the computer — so you listen on the PC without the rig blaring, and your decoders never go quiet.

## 📱 ECHOCAT on iOS and Android — 1-touch connect at first launch

The phone app finds your shack on the network and connects with a **single tap** — no typing IP addresses, no certificate wrestling. Discover your rig, approve the pairing once, and you're operating. ECHOCAT is now live for **both iOS and Android**.

## ⌨️ Unlimited CW macros

CW macros are no longer capped at five. **Add as many as you need (up to 25)** with simple +/− controls, just like voice macros — and they sync to the ECHOCAT phone app.

## 🕐 One-click clock sync for FT8/JTCAT

A PC clock that's off by even a couple of seconds silently kills FT8 decoding while everything *looks* fine. POTACAT now **detects the drift and fixes it for you** — the "Sync now" button repairs and starts the Windows time service (not just a resync that quietly fails), and the warning banner stays put until the clock is actually corrected.

## 🛠️ Fixes & polish

- **Flex Rig settings:** repaired a bug where saving a Flex rig could break SmartSDR detection; existing setups self-heal on launch.
- **ECHOCAT discovery** failures are no longer silent — the desktop now logs exactly why a phone couldn't connect.
- Quieter logs and assorted reliability improvements.
